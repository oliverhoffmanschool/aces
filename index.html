♦ About Us ♦

Entrepreneur: Leo Cook

Our Story: Founded by Leo Cook, a high school student with a passion for fashion and individuality, Aces was born out of a desire to create something different. The idea for Aces came while playing cards with friends—he saw the potential to blend classic imagery with everyday wear. With an eye for unique patterns and a love for self-expression, he launched Aces to make jorts cool again.
